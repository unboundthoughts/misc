🌱 Daily Work Guide

Small steps. Calm mind. One day at a time.

🟢 Step 1: Talk

Go to 1–2 departments and ask:

  • "Do you need any help?"
  • "Can I assist with anything?"

🟡 Step 2: Write

Write simple notes:

  • Department Name
  • Who you spoke to
  • What they said
  • What you understood

You don’t need to understand everything.

🔵 Step 3: Email

Send ONE simple email:

Today I spoke to:
- [Department] → [Name of the person] → [What they said]

Summary:
What I understood

Tomorrow:
Next step

✅ Do

  • Small steps
  • Stay calm
  • One task at a time

❌ Don’t

  • Overthink
  • Rush
  • Do too much

❤️ Go slow. One small thing is enough.
